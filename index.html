Application Process

How to Apply

Four steps from application to selection.

01

Portal Opens

Explore available roles, review requirements, and prepare your application.

02

Submit Application

Complete and submit the centralized application form before the deadline. Late or incomplete submissions will not be considered.

03

Interview

Interviews are conducted by the respective MCVP and NST Leads for the function you applied to.

04

Selection

The National Support Team for Term 26.27 is announced and onboarding begins.

Rules and Guidelines

Eligibility

Open to all active AIESEC in Sri Lanka members. You must be an active member of an AIESEC Local Committee in Sri Lanka.

Submission Format

Role-specific answers must be submitted through the centralized form or as an attached PDF booklet. All mandatory fields must be completed.

Deadline

Late or incomplete submissions will not be evaluated. Submit well before the deadline.

Multiple Applications

You may apply for multiple roles across different functions. Each application should be specific to the role you are applying for.
